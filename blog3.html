What Is Creator Economy?

Creator Economy, which is also termed as Gig/ Passion Economy is a new catch phrase nowadays for the people who are monetizing their interests. Turning your audience into a strong community of your own to engage with is like creating your own cult, an Identity.

For example, you are a full time Software Developer, and in your free time you are also a Graphic Designer. What would be the next best thing? To engage with the audience and monetize your work, right? This allows the said person to have a nice blanket of extra earnings for the week or month. Keeping their lives afloat and comfortable, which leads to Passion Economy.

Since 2018, this has finally caught some speed with Conventional Economy, thanks to the revolutionization of the Internet across the globe.

At present, as reported by Singal Fire, approximately 4% of the total existing Creators are Professionals, and the rest 96% are Amateurs who are hustling this as side business. This particular statistic itself confirms the power of Content Creation which is uplifting so many individuals across many homes.

The year 2020 pushed so many talented individuals in their homes for longer than general 8-10 hours, Pre-Pandemic. This nutshell created space for exploration, growth, more time for oneself, leading to creation of a strong influx of aspiring content creators. Platforms have helped many people, especially the younger generation, to use them as a way to express themselves, their emotions, thoughts, opinions, beautiful stories, and so forth.

Today, making an Instagram Reel takes no more than 20 min, same goes with TikTok. People took advantage of this, and more are working on creating it as a sustainable side hustle. For example, one person I know works in a corporation. In mid-2020, she started her Business Instagram Page for her paintings, which now stays active with 10k followers, and growth chart seems to be only upwards.

The online platform has given an outlet to so many people to express, to thrive, to generate content which they always wanted to do.

This wave of creation has also been because of major consumption of the said art. In general terms, what we have learnt in our school days, Supply and Demand. The demand for consumption of digital art is nowhere near to be slowing down anyway soon. This has boosted people around the globe to create more. Not only that, but the way MainStream Media is now investing in Content Creators to collaborate and work with them to share their product across the market has also taken a steep rise. Media Houses, Corporates are now more aware of how people are consuming information these days, through social media. Artists are sponsored by Guitar Manufacturer to perform on one of the reels, YouTube Video with there product, so as it can reach a mass consumer base, beyond boundaries of geo location. This helps both the creator to earn money, and Companies to build a market in the Creators Community.

Creator Economy is solely based on creating your own community, a niche but your own, like an Identity. This allows creators to open multiple avenues to engage with them through Merchandises, Offers, Sales, Meet-Ups, Collaboration, and much more. Creators can negotiaṣte with the world through their size of the community they build and truly work as a small business.
