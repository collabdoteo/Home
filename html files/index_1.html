About us

We at Collab.eo want to help people come together, to create something beautiful.

Collab.eo's vision is to strengthen the meaning of collaboration by giving a platform for both collaborators and upcoming artists, to work together. We at Collab.eo have one goal of building realities, not dreams.

"Generally, the cream of the cake gets to see the shine of beauty, but we forget, what we really enjoy is the sponge of layers within that imperfect cake."
